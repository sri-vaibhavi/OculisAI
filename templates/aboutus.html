About OCULIS AI

OCULIS AI is an advanced medical web application designed to support healthcare professionals in the early detection and monitoring of retinal diseases. Using state-of-the-art artificial intelligence, our platform analyzes high-resolution retinal fundus images with exceptional accuracy and reliability.

The system is capable of identifying early signs of diabetic retinopathy, glaucoma, age-related macular degeneration, and several other vision-threatening conditions. These insights allow clinicians to make faster, more informed decisions that lead to improved patient outcomes.

Our mission is to harness AI technology to prevent avoidable blindness by making retinal screening more accessible, accurate, and efficient for everyone.

Our Services

AI-powered retinal fundus image analysis
This service uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze retinal fundus images with high precision. It detects diabetic retinopathy, glaucoma, cataract, and other retinal disorders at an early stage. The system highlights affected regions for improved clinical interpretation. It significantly reduces manual screening time. AI-driven analysis ensures consistent and reliable results. This service supports ophthalmologists in confident diagnosis and treatment planning. It is ideal for hospitals, clinics, and screening programs.
Early detection of diabetic retinopathy
This service focuses on identifying early retinal changes caused by diabetes. It detects microaneurysms, hemorrhages, and vascular abnormalities before symptoms appear. Early diagnosis allows timely medical intervention and prevents vision loss. The system supports routine diabetic screening programs. AI ensures uniform evaluation across patients. This service plays a key role in proactive eye care and long-term vision preservation.
